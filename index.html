Bite-size Shift

Welcome to Bite-size Shift!

My journal of learning web developer, hoping to make a shift to the industry.

This is something big, a life-changing period of my life. At first, I was amazed, I was overwhelmed, I doubt my self, I want to go back.

But deep inside, I know I want to give it a try! Time is not going backward anyway, better try it now than later.

For those taking this path, drop me a Hi! I would need every support and help in this journey.

For those who consider make the shift to web development but still cautiously observing options available for you, I am gladly share this experience with you. Feel free to ask anything. Literally, anything.

Enjoy your stay!
